Art is This #2, Digital Video

This project is a continuation of my project 2. It's meant to bluntly pose the question of what art can be. The words shift around the screen in different orders creating phrases such as "Is This Art" or "Art Is This." The dark, deep colors and pulsating screen give the piece a cynical tone. The retro film look is a homage to early video art which experienced scrutiny in its qualifications in being considered high art. In this piece, I try to portray the art world from an outsider looking in. Words such as pretentious, glamourous, and pompous come to mind when I imagine outsiders thinking about "high art."
