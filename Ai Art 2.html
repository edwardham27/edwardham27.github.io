Raw Inspiration, Interactive Gifs

In this work I continue my process of overlaying videos with a painterly style using ai image processing. In this iteration, I implemented an interactive element in which you hover over the video to switch between the raw video and its ai processed counterpart. The concept I explore is how artists can become enveloped in their work or medium. I wanted to capture that feeling of full immersion in a world seen through the lens of an artist. It's a visual representation of a painter's raw imagination before it takes a static form on a canvas. This is accomplished through digitally animated versions of traditional painting styles.

Instructions: Hover over the image to reveal the ai processed version.
