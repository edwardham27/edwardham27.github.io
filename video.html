180 Days, Video

This video project was edited in adobe premiere. I split the rgb channels and offset them slightly to create the retro vhs look. The theme of the video covers the potential of college life to become reptitive and disorienting. The audio clips that repeat along with the video form a rythm as the video progresses. I drew inspiration from retro VHS tapes for the aesthetic and my own college experience for the concept.
