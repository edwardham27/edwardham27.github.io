Lonely City Shapes, Digital Video, 1920x1080, 16 seconds

This piece is a conceptual piece on the possibility of AR as a means of architectural or urban design. I was inspired by my time in art 107 in which we explored ar and its potential uses or implications. In this project I learned and used 3d camera motion tracking in after effects to composite motion graphics onto found footage. I created moving shapes and lines throughout a night city to see what the world would look like with public ai art and design. I wanted to convey a feeling of wonder and magic while retaining a modernist aesthetic.
