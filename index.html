Edward Ham | Digital Media Arts Portfolio

This website is a collection of student works and assignments created by Edward Ham. Much of his work takes interest in bringing retro aesthetics into the forefront of modern digital media. He also takes heavy inspiration from pop culture and early pop art. This collection focuses on his development of new technical skills and the exploration of new mediums. Edward's prefered medium is video and motion graphics. He explores what it means to be an artist through his works. He questions what is and what is not art by contextualizing and comparing the new to the old. He celebrates the clash between the new and cutting edge against dated or classical styles.
