Art Building, Digital Video

This video project was created using the ai tool called ebsynth and edited in adobe premiere. I took original footage of the art building and processed a deep image replacement using Roy Lichtenstein's Drowning Girl. This piece is meant to reflect a dream like state within the school environment. The art building is where students go to chase their dreams.
